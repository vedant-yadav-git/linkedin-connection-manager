Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Connection Manager

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

LinkedIn Connection Manager collects and stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • LinkedIn profile URLs you choose to save
  • Names and titles from LinkedIn profiles
  • Notes and tags you add to connections
  • Follow-up reminder dates you set

2. How We Use Your Information

All information is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not:

  • Send your data to any external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Collect analytics or usage data

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. It remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your browser's security. We recommend:

  • Using a secure password for your device
  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Using Chrome's built-in security features

5. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Connections" button in Settings
  • Uninstalling the extension
  • Clearing your browser's storage data

6. Third-Party Services

This extension only interacts with LinkedIn.com to extract publicly visible profile information. We do not use any other third-party services.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
